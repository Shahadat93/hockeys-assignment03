Clients Question

Any questions? We've got it covered! Please see our frequently asked questions below.

Is this club just for elite players?

No! Players of all ages, abilities and experience are welcome here. Whether you’re a beginner, a social player or an aspiring international, there is a team for you at the club (and a warm off-pitch welcome for social members and Colts parents!). We have 20 adult teams and 50 junior teams – there is something for everyone and we’d love to see you at the club.

I've never played hockey before. Is it possible to try out before I decide whether to join?

Absolutely. Our Back 2 Hockey programme is the perfect way to give hockey a try before you decide whether you’d like to join a senior team. Find out more information here or email us for more details.

Do you Provide Goalkeeper coaching?

Goalkeepers have access to specialist GK sessions during the season.

When are trials?

The club’s annual Play Day is held in early September every year. This is the best way to meet people and find the team that suits you best. Keep an eye out for details on the club website and social media feeds.

Who are the coaches?

We have a huge team of experienced and inspirational coaches at Surbiton HC, including qualified coaches and current international players.

When is the hockey season?

The senior hockey season runs from September through until March/April. For adult players, there is also various informal summer hockey available during the off season, including a 7-aside summer league.

The junior season runs from September until March, with some breaks during school holidays. We also provide a Summer Programme for juniors (which is open to non-members).
